About Us

Vundla legacy T/A KFL construction is a leading construction company operating in South Africa with over 10 years of industry experience including its operations before being registered. Since its registration in 2019 the company has maintained a firm commitment to providing quality construction services, showcasing excellence and innovation in every project. The company specializes in both commercial and residential projects, ranging from office buildings and shopping centers to luxury houses and apartments.

Vundla legacy was founded by Wellington Vundla, a man with a vision of creating a construction company that values integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction above all else. Over the years, the company has grown exponentially, now employing over 50 dedicated professionals and having completed many projects across the state.
